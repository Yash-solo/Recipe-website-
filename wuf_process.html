Recipe : Belgian waffles

Step by Step full process

• Preheat:Get your waffle iron hot according to the manufacturer's instructions
• Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
• Mix Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks, milk, melted butter (or oil), and vanilla together.
• Whip Egg Whites: In another clean bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form. This is the secret to extra fluffy waffles.
• Combine: Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined (some lumps are fine). Gently fold in the whipped egg whites.


• Cook:Pour batter onto the hot waffle iron. Cook for 4–6 minutes or until golden brown and crisp.


• Serve: Top immediately with fresh berries and a generous dollop of whipped cream


Preparation time:30min

Serving-size: 2 plates
