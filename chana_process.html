Recipe : Chana masala

Step by Step full process

• Sauté Aromatics:Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds; once they sizzle, add the onions and cook until softened and starting to brown (about 3–5 minutes). [1, 2]
• Add Ginger & Garlic: Stir in the ginger and garlic and sauté until fragrant (about 1 minute). [1, 2]
• Toast Spices: Add the ground coriander, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Stir constantly for about 30 seconds to release their aroma without burning them. [1, 2]
• Simmer: Stir in the tomatoes and chickpeas. Add approximately 1 cup of water or vegetable stock. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10–15 minutes until the sauce thickens and the chickpeas are tender. [1, 2, 3]
• Finish:Stir in the garam masala and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice. Garnish generously with chopped cilantro before serving


Preparation time:30min

Serving-size: 2 plates
