Recipe : Maalpua

Ingredients-list:-

semolina, sugar (if using in batter), cardamom powder, and fennel seeds,ghee and milk

Step by Step full process

• Prepare the Batter: In a bowl, mix flour, semolina, sugar (if using in batter), cardamom powder, and fennel seeds. Gradually whisk in milk to form a smooth, lump-free batter with a pouring consistency similar to pancakes. Let the batter rest for 30 minutes to 2 hours. [1, 2, 3, 4, 6]
• Make the Sugar Syrup: Combine sugar and water in a pan. Boil until the sugar dissolves and the syrup becomes slightly sticky or reaches a "one-string" consistency. Add saffron and extra cardamom for flavor. [1, 2, 3, 4]
• Fry the Malpua: Heat ghee or oil in a broad, shallow pan on medium-low heat. Pour a ladleful of batter into the center; it will spread into a round disc on its own. Fry until the edges are golden brown and crispy, then flip to cook the other side. [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]
• Soak and Serve: Immediately dip the hot, fried malpuas into the warm sugar syrup. Let them soak for 2–10 minutes to absorb the sweetness. [1, 2, 3]
• Garnish: Serve warm, garnished with almond slivers or pistachio flakes. [1, 2]


Preparation time:2hr

Serving-size: 2plates
