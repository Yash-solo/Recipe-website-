Recipe : Guliapaa

Ingredients-list:-

rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds,the poha,green chilies, coriander, and curry leaves

Step by Step full process

• Soak: Wash and soak the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 3 to 4 hours. Soak the poha separately in water for about 30 minutes. [1]
• Grind & Ferment: Drain the water and grind the rice and dal mixture along with the softened poha into a smooth paste (similar to a dosa batter consistency). Add salt, pour the batter into a large bowl, and let it ferment in a warm place for 6 to 8 hours (or overnight). [1, 2, 3]
• Mix: : Once fermented, gently stir in the finely chopped onions, green chilies, coriander, and curry leaves to the batter. [1, 2]
• Cook: : Heat a Guliyappa (Paddu) pan on medium heat. Add a few drops of oil into each cavity. Pour the batter into each mold until it is about ¾ full. [1, 2, 3, 4]
• Flip: Cover with a lid and cook for 3 minutes until the bottom turns golden brown. Use a wooden skewer or spoon to carefully flip them over, and cook the other side until crispy. [1, 2]


• Serve: Serve hot with Hurigadale Chutney or sambar. [1]


Preparation time:1hr

Serving-size: 4 plates
