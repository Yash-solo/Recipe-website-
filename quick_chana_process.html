Recipe : Quick Chana masala

Step by Step full process

• Base:1 large onion (diced), 2–4 cloves garlic (minced), and 1-inch fresh ginger (grated).
• Chickpeas: 2 cans (15 oz each) or 1 cup dried chickpeas (soaked overnight and boiled).
• Tomato: 1 can (14 oz) crushed or chopped tomatoes, or 2 fresh Roma tomatoes.
• Spices: Cumin seeds, ground coriander, turmeric, chili powder (Kashmiri or cayenne), and garam masala.
• Finishers:Fresh cilantro (coriander), lemon juice, and salt to taste


Preparation time:30min

Serving-size: 1 bowl
